About Me

Hello, and welcome to my site. My name is Jennifer Daeumler, and I was born and raised in Saint Louis, Missouri. I was a math teacher for three years before making the leap to become a full-stack developer.

My journey to becoming a full-stack devloper has been a result of trying to find a career I am passionate about. I recieved my B.S. in Speech Pathology and Master's in Education from Fontbonne University. I knew I had a love for problem solving, analyzing data, and creating projects, but never knew how I could use those skills and interests in a career. After months and months of research, soul searching, and Googling, I discovered coding bootcamps. I utilized CodeAcademy for a while to see if I would enjoy coding, and I became obsessed. I am looking forward to building my coding skill set, and finding a career as a developer.
